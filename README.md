The bike sales project consisted of 113,037 transactions. I cleaned and formatted the dataset by removing duplicates and standardising formats such as currency and dates. I also used formulas to calculate cost, revenue, and net profit. I then created pivot tables and pivot charts to analyse annual revenue and cost over a five-year period, as well as product revenue by country and customer demographics. Over those five years, sales increased rapidly to reach a peak of $29.7 million in 2021. The United States recorded the highest volume of bikes sold, contributing the majority of global revenue. Additionally, adult shoppers dominate the customer base, with adults making up 50%, young adults 36%, and youth 14%.

Tableau 

I built this Tableau dashboard to analyse spotify's listener trends. I used aggregation functions such as average and sums and linear regression trend lines to create graphs to identify trends and patterns between key music features and popularity. During my analysis, i found that there is a positive correlation between track popularity and danceability but it's not a causation. Additionally pop, rap and rock are the most popular genres of music consumed by listeners. And tracks that have a duration between 3.5 - 4.5 minutes are the more popular with spotify listeners, compared with longer or shorter tracks. These findings would help spotify make more intentional decisions on marketing particular genres of music such as pop and rock and using these findings to increase user engagement. 



PowerBi
I developed this interactive Power BI dashboard to explore retail transaction data and transform them into meaningful business insights.I used power query to clean and format the raw data, then I created a decomposition tree, to investigate total retail revenue by drilling down into different regions, customer segments, and individual countries to identify the factors affecting sales.For example, one path through the analysis shows that the Central region generates the highest overall sales at $312K, while drilling down further reveals that the Consumer segment in Germany contributes more than $75K in revenue. Because the dashboard is interactive, users can explore different combinations of filters and dimensions to uncover additional trends and compare performance across markets.Throughout this project, I strengthened my skills in data modelling, data analysis, interactive dashboard development, root-cause analysis, data visualisation, and business intelligence. The dashboard demonstrates my ability to build interactive reports that allow stakeholders to explore data independently, uncover meaningful insights, and make more informed business decisions.
