# 📝 Project Overview

This repository contains **two Microsoft Excel data analysis projects** focused on retail and bike sales data.

The objective was to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights by cleaning datasets, analysing customer behaviour, identifying revenue trends, and building interactive visualisations.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Clean and prepare raw datasets for analysis
- Analyse customer purchasing behaviour
- Evaluate product performance
- Identify sales and revenue trends
- Present findings through Excel dashboards and charts

# 💡 Key Findings

## 🛍️ Customer Purchasing Behaviour

The retail analysis showed that **clothing** was the most frequently purchased product category across both male and female customers.

Although clothing generated the highest sales volume, it produced the lowest overall revenue, indicating a lower average selling price than other product categories.

### Business Value

Understanding purchasing behaviour enables organisations to:

- Optimise pricing strategies
- Evaluate product profitability
- Improve inventory planning
- Increase revenue opportunities

---

## 📈 Revenue Trends

The bike sales analysis identified consistent revenue growth across five years, reaching approximately **$29.7 million** in **2021**.

Additional insights included:

- 🇺🇸 United States generated the highest revenue
- 👥 Adult customers represented approximately 50% of total purchases

### Business Value

These findings help organisations:

- Identify high-performing markets
- Understand their primary customer base
- Support future marketing and sales strategies
- Prioritise business investment
